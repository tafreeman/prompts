# Business & Operations Prompts

Give every stakeholder update, operating review, and transformation workstream a consistent template. These prompts pair especially well with M365 Copilot (Word, PowerPoint, Outlook) and ChatGPT for fast rewrites, structured summaries, and action trackers.

## In This Section

| Scenario | Prompt | Output |
| :--- |--------| :--- |
| Executive briefings | [Board Update](/prompts/business/board-update) | Slide-ready summary with KPIs and risks |
| Change programs | [Organizational Change Manager](/prompts/business/organizational-change-manager) | Stakeholder map, messaging, adoption plan |
| Delivery cadence | [Agile Sprint Planner](/prompts/business/agile-sprint-planner) | Sprint goals, risks, demo story |
| Sales follow-up | [Sales Objection Handler](/prompts/business/sales-objection-handler) | Tailored responses + CTA |
| Post-project wrap | [Project Closure Specialist](/prompts/business/project-closure-specialist) | Lessons learned, metrics, thank-you note |

## Quick Picks

- **Need a clean meeting recap?** Use [Meeting Summary](/prompts/business/meeting-summary) with Teams/Outlook transcripts.
- **Budget pressure?** [Budget & Cost Controller](/prompts/business/budget-and-cost-controller) highlights savings, trade-offs, and owner actions.
- **Preparing a pitch deck?** Pair [Pitch Deck Generator](/prompts/business/pitch-deck-generator) with [Client Presentation Designer](/prompts/business/client-presentation-designer) for storyline + visuals.
