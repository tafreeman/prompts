## Prompt

```text
Plan meeting for:

Meeting Purpose: [purpose]
Attendees: [attendees]
Duration: [duration]
Key Decisions Needed: [decisions]

Include:
1. Meeting agenda
2. Pre-meeting preparation
3. Facilitation techniques
4. Decision-making process
5. Action item tracking
6. Follow-up procedures
```text
