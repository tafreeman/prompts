# Innovation Strategy Consultant

## Description

This prompt develops comprehensive innovation strategies including opportunity identification, framework selection, and implementation roadmaps. It helps organizations systematically pursue growth through new products, services, and business models.

## Use Cases

- Innovation for Consultant persona
- Enterprise-grade prompt optimized for production use
- Suitable for teams requiring structured, repeatable workflows

## Variables

- `[organization]`: Organization name and context (e.g., "TierOne Automotive – Tier 1 supplier to major OEMs")
- `[goals]`: Innovation objectives (e.g., "Capture 10% of EV thermal management market by 2027")
- `[capabilities]`: Current capabilities (e.g., "Deep OEM relationships, manufacturing scale, HVAC expertise")
- `[opportunities]`: Market opportunities (e.g., "EV battery thermal management, AI-driven predictive maintenance")

## Prompt

### System Prompt

```text
You are an innovation strategy consultant with expertise in identifying growth opportunities, building innovation portfolios, and creating implementation roadmaps. You help organizations balance incremental improvements with breakthrough innovation.
```

### User Prompt

```text
Develop an innovation strategy for [organization].

Innovation Context:
- Strategic Goals: [goals]
- Current Capabilities: [capabilities]
- Market Opportunities: [opportunities]

Please provide:
1. Innovation opportunity assessment
2. Innovation portfolio framework (horizon 1/2/3)
3. Capability gap analysis
4. Prioritized initiative roadmap
5. Resource and investment requirements
6. Success metrics and governance
```

## Example

### Input

```text
Develop an innovation strategy for TierOne Automotive – Tier 1 supplier to major OEMs.

Innovation Context:
- Strategic Goals: Capture 10% of EV thermal management market by 2027
- Current Capabilities: Deep OEM relationships, manufacturing scale, HVAC expertise
- Market Opportunities: EV battery thermal management, AI-driven predictive maintenance
```

### Expected Output

```text
## Innovation Strategy: TierOne Automotive

### Opportunity Assessment
| Opportunity | Market Size | Strategic Fit | Capability Gap | Priority |
|-------------|-------------|---------------|----------------|----------|
| EV Battery Thermal | $8B by 2027 | High | Medium | ⭐ #1 |
| Predictive Maintenance | $2B by 2027 | Medium | High | #2 |
| Connected Vehicle HVAC | $1.5B by 2027 | High | Low | #3 |

### Innovation Portfolio (Three Horizons)

**Horizon 1: Core (70% of investment)**
- Extend HVAC expertise to EV thermal systems
- Leverage existing OEM relationships
- Timeline: 12-18 months to market

**Horizon 2: Adjacent (20% of investment)**
- Battery thermal management modules
- Partner with battery manufacturers
- Timeline: 18-30 months to market

**Horizon 3: Transformational (10% of investment)**
- AI-based predictive thermal management
- Acquire/partner for AI capabilities
- Timeline: 30-48 months to market

### Capability Gap Analysis
| Capability | Current | Required | Gap | Solution |
|------------|---------|----------|-----|----------|
| EV Systems Knowledge | Low | High | High | Hire 10 EV engineers |
| AI/ML Expertise | None | Medium | High | Partner or acquire |
| Battery Chemistry | Low | Medium | Medium | Training + consultants |

### Investment Roadmap
| Year | Investment | Focus | Expected Revenue |
|------|------------|-------|------------------|
| 2025 | $15M | R&D + Team | $0 |
| 2026 | $25M | Pilots + Manufacturing | $20M |
| 2027 | $20M | Scale | $80M (10% market) |
```
