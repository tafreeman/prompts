## Using Business Prompts

1. **Select a prompt** for your business need
2. **Provide context** about your company, industry, and situation
3. **Fill in the details** (company size, market, goals)
4. **Review and adapt** the output to your specific situation

## Tips for Business Users

- Be specific about your company size and industry
- Include relevant metrics and data when available
- Mention constraints (budget, timeline, resources)
- Clarify who will use the output (executives, team, stakeholders)
- Provide context about current challenges
- Specify your decision timeframe
