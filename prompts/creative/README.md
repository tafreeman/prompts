## Using Creative Prompts

1. **Select a prompt** for your content need
2. **Define your audience** clearly
3. **Specify tone and style** you want
4. **Include SEO keywords** if relevant
5. **Review and refine** the output

## Tips for Creative Professionals

- Always specify your target audience
- Define the tone (professional, casual, humorous, etc.)
- Include examples of style you like when possible
- Mention word count or length requirements
- Add SEO keywords naturally
- Specify call-to-action if needed
- Provide brand guidelines or voice requirements

## Getting Better Results

**Be specific about:**

- Who you're writing for
- What action you want readers to take
- Your brand's personality
- Format requirements
- Key messages to include

**Iterate with phrases like:**

- "Make it more conversational"
- "Add more data/statistics"
- "Shorten by 30%"
- "Make the CTA stronger"
- "Use more storytelling"
