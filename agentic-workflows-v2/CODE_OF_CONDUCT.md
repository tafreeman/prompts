# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and collaborative community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and professional language.
- Assume good intent and give constructive feedback.
- Focus on technical issues, not personal attacks.
- Respect different viewpoints and experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Doxxing, intimidation, or threats.
- Repeated off-topic disruption in issues, PRs, or discussions.
- Sharing private information without explicit permission.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other collaboration channels.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:
- Open a private security-style report following `SECURITY.md`, or
- Contact maintainers via the support route in `SUPPORT.md`.

Include links, screenshots, and context where possible.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take actions including warning, content removal, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans based on severity and repeated behavior.

## Attribution

This policy is adapted for this repository and aligns with common open source community conduct standards.
